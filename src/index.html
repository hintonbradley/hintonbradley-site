About Me

During my employment in Music Administration, I rediscovered my interest in finding solutions to problems. It wasn't long before I decided to pursue an education in web development. In June of 2019 I graduated as part of the 17th cohort of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program.

Since then, while living in San Francisco and Napa Valley, I have refined my front-end skills working with client-side libraries and frameworks like jQuery, Bootstrap, Angular, Vue and React. I have implemented responsive design building apps that use various relational and non-relational databases, as well as web services by writing clean, maintainable, composable code that is easily adaptable to changing requirements.
